Our Story

Immigration Records
started as a question.

What would a music company look like if it was built for artists who come from somewhere — not from the industry's idea of what Latin music should sound like?

The music industry is good at many things. Developing emerging artists from outside its traditional networks is not one of them. We built Immigration Records to change that.

We started as a boutique agency. We grew into a full-service music company. And now, with UNSIN Labs, we're building the AI tools that will help the next generation of Latin creatives compete without compromising who they are.
